⚔️ NAP Rules

1. No Attacks on NAP Alliances

No city attacks, scouting, or rallies against any NAP alliance or their academy alliances.

Punishment:
  • The attacker must allow the victim to attack their empty city to recover lost resources.
  • Compensation must be witnessed by an Alliance R4/R5.
  • Additional punishment may be imposed by NAP leadership depending on the severity of the violation.

2. No Poaching

Poaching members from NAP alliances is prohibited. A respectful invitation (without threats, deception, or repeated pressure) will not be considered poaching. Poaching complaints must be reported within 5 days of the incident.

💡 Exception: Temporary player transfers for KvK are allowed after informing the NAP group in advance.
Punishment: To be determined by NAP leadership based on the circumstances.

3. Academy Alliances

Each NAP alliance may have one farm/academy alliance. Main alliances may not interfere in academy outpost or sanctuary battles by transferring top players into the academy for the purpose of gaining an advantage.

Punishment: If an academy captures a sanctuary through such interference, the main alliance will forfeit its right to that sanctuary during the next sanctuary cycle.

4. Banner Blocking

Attacking or intentionally blocking the banners of a NAP alliance is prohibited.

Punishment: Any banner intentionally blocking an outpost or territory expansion will be removed, either by the responsible alliance's R4/R5 or through NAP-approved destruction.

5. Harassment & Doxxing

Harassment, personal attacks, threats, and doxxing are strictly prohibited.

Punishment:
  • Permanent removal from NAP protection and alliances.
  • Additional actions may be determined by NAP leadership.
6. Enforcement NAP leadership reserves the right to investigate incidents and issue additional penalties when necessary to maintain fairness and cooperation among all member alliances.

👑 King's Responsibilities

1. Appointments

All kingdom appointments, except Minister of Justice, will be available to all NAP members through an application-based process. The King and appointed officers ensure fair and efficient distribution.

2. Minister of Justice

The Minister of Justice position will be used to enable automatic appointments. This ensures members of the NAP 5 alliances receive requested titles automatically.

3. State Buffs

During events, the King must activate appropriate King Skills (State Buffs): Troop training, Construction, Research, and Combat. Must be activated in a timely manner.

4. Kingdom First

The King is expected to act in the best interest of the kingdom as a whole, ensuring fairness, cooperation, and equal opportunities for all NAP alliances.

⚖️ Castle Jail Term Protocols

Objective

To maintain fairness, dignity, and order within the NAP. No player shall be placed in jail without following the procedures. The King & MOJ shall be added to the NAP group to coordinate effectively.

⚠️ Emergency Exception Clause If the President believes immediate action is necessary because a player has gone rogue and poses an active threat to kingdom stability, the player may be jailed temporarily before the formal process begins, followed by an immediate NAP Council review.
Due Process Workflow

Players seeking action must first contact Alliance Leadership to raise it in the NAP Group.

⚖️ Sentencing Guidelines
  • Jail Duration: Determined by the NAP Council based on the severity and frequency of the offense.
  • Type of Jail Term: Specific conditions and enforcement measures shall be determined by the NAP Council.
🕵️ Definition of Rogue Behavior
  • Unjustified Aggression: Attacks without a valid cause.
  • Agreement Violation: Attacking protected targets.
  • Indiscriminate Rampage: Widespread swift attacks.
  • Defiance: Ignoring direct stand-down orders.
⚖️ Grounds for Imprisonment
I. Harassment Language intended to intimidate, threaten, or harm mental well-being.
II. Malicious Defamation Knowingly spreading false accusations against an alliance.
III. Disruptive Behavior Provoking internal disputes or encouraging rebellion against leadership.
IV. Discriminatory Hate Speech Use of racial, ethnic, religious, or discriminatory slurs.
V. Persistent Non-Compliance Repeated violations of NAP rules despite warnings or action.
Guiding Principle: The purpose of imprisonment is to preserve kingdom stability and accountability, not to punish players unfairly.

🏟️ Arena Agreement

Objective

To help all members of the kingdom maximize Arena rankings and rewards through cooperation rather than internal competition.

Arena Rules:

• Players must NOT attack members of their own kingdom in the Arena.

• If no external targets are available, refresh the Arena opponent list instead of attacking a fellow kingdom member.

• This applies across all NAP alliances and their academy alliances.

Reporting Violations If attacked, report the incident to your Alliance R4/R5. The matter will be raised with the offending alliance for resolution.
Enforcement Policy • First offense: Warning from Alliance Leadership.
• Repeated/Intentional: Action determined by NAP Council.

📊 KvK Scoring & Rotations Grid

Scoring Basis

KvK Prep Phase scores are normalized to alliance power at the end of each event, accumulating through the Transfer Manager rotation cycle. This combined ranking serves as the official "KvK Ranking".

Normalized Score Formula
Normalized Score = (KvK Prep Score × 1000) ÷ Alliance Power
👑 Rotation Systems
High Kingships Rotated strictly based on the accumulated KvK Ranking. Does not count toward regular rotation.
Regular Castles Rotated among all NAP alliances following the baseline power rankings.
Sanctuary & Forts Selection priority order directly follows the latest updated KvK ranking.
🔄 Transfer Manager Rotation

Cycle duration is structured as NAP-1 (e.g., NAP4 results in 3 rotations). Rotated based on KvK Ranking. Special invitations prioritized by ranking; unused invitations by Day 2 are reallocated by the KTC.
